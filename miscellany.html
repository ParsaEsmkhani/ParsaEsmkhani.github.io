5 Movie Recommendations

  • The Lives of Others (2006) A truly moving and thought-provoking movie.
  • Monty Python and the Holy Grail (1975) The only movie that still, on the fourth watch, made me consistently laugh. Invaluable comedy.
  • Funny Games (2007) Unlike any movie you've watched before.
  • Benny's Video (1992) My favourite movie ending ever.
  • I'm Still Here (2024) The movie that made me cry the most; it hit too close to home.

You'll notice 3 of the 5 are German movies. German cinema is a beauty. Funny Games was made in both German and English with different casts; I think the English version is just as good.
